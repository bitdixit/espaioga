yWakeUp - Yoga Wake Up

Version: WUP "Agasana"

Relaxing morning yoga timer

Yoga wake up is a specially designed application for those who want to practice yoga in the morning and don't want to be late at work.

Start the application and touch the screen to begin with the practice that is initially set at 15 minutes. Each time you touch the screen you will add 5 minutes to your practice.
After the time has been set, a bell will indicate that the practice starts.

Later on, the bell will sound when 10, 5 and 1 minute are left to finish your practice. This will help you planify your session.

Keep the screen pressed to stop the timer.

Some parameters can be changed via the Behaviour menu.
